About the Gym

This is Hart Gym, BYUI’s fitness and training center. Here you will find a variety of equipment, resources, and the environment you need to achieve your best. Whether you’re a casual newcomer, or an experienced lifter, this is the destination for you. The best part? It’s free for students!
